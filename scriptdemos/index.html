Capturing Masterclasses

Masterclass.com offers courses on diverse subjects, taught by well-known experts. Each course presents a sequence of lessons via streaming video.

In brief, my program visits every lesson in my course list and acquires each video in full, creating an organized collection of videos on my computer for my own private, personal offline viewing.

I use Selenium WebDriver for browser automation, FFmpeg for media acquisition and processing, and pywin32 for Windows GUI automation.
